Family Tree of Parts for Manufacturing

  • Category: Web design
  • Project date: 20 Sep, 2022

This apps makes a Family Tree of Parts for Manufacturing based in a database with parts information and their relationship with another parts

This application is designed as a guide for workers who assemble composite products, having information on quantities and what parts it has. To generate the trees, this App is based on the parts already loaded, their relationship with other parts, such as what they make up or that composes them, their materials and finishes. Specific data such as drawing codes, part code, 3D model images are also saved. It has an additional extension that allows you to interact with a rendered 3D model in the browser.
